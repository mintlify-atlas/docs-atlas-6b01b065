## Progression overview

Bards RPG features five distinct equipment tiers, each offering increased power and new capabilities. Progression follows traditional Minecraft material tiers with unique Bard-themed variants.

## Armor progression

### Tier 1: Entertainer's Garb

**Materials**: Wool (any color)

**Base Protection**: 1 / 3 / 2 / 1 (Head / Chest / Legs / Feet)

**Bonuses per piece**:
- +15% Arcane spell power
- +15% Healing spell power
- +3% Movement speed

**Full set bonuses**:
- +60% total spell power (both schools)
- +12% total movement speed

**Enchantability**: 9

**Durability multiplier**: 10

**Crafting**: Standard wool-based recipes

---

### Tier 2: Troubadour's Garb

**Materials**: Wool

**Base Protection**: 1 / 3 / 2 / 1

**Bonuses per piece**:
- +20% Arcane spell power
- +20% Healing spell power
- +4% Movement speed

**Full set bonuses**:
- +80% total spell power
- +16% total movement speed

**Enchantability**: 10

**Durability multiplier**: 20

---

### Tier 3: Netherite Troubadour's Garb

**Materials**: Netherite Ingots (repair material)

**Base Protection**: 1 / 3 / 2 / 1

**Bonuses per piece**:
- +25% Arcane spell power
- +25% Healing spell power
- +5% Movement speed

**Full set bonuses**:
- +100% total spell power
- +20% total movement speed

**Enchantability**: 15

**Durability multiplier**: 30

**Sound**: Netherite equip sound

---

### Tier 5: Storyteller's Garb

**Requirements**: Requires **Armory RPGs** mod to be installed

**Materials**: Netherite Ingots (repair material)

**Base Protection**: 1 / 3 / 2 / 1

**Bonuses per piece**:
- +30% Arcane spell power
- +30% Healing spell power
- +6% Movement speed

**Full set bonuses**:
- +120% total spell power
- +24% total movement speed

**Enchantability**: 18

**Durability multiplier**: 40

**Piece names**: Storyteller Hat, Storyteller Tunic, Storyteller Trousers, Storyteller Boots

### Lyres

Healing-focused staves with enhanced support capabilities.

**Base stats**: -2.2 attack speed, 3.0 base damage, healing staff type

**Power distribution**: 50% Arcane multiplier, 150% Healing multiplier (compared to Lutes)

| T0 | Golden Lyre | Gold Ingot | +1.75 | +5.25 | Golden |

| T4 | Valkyrie Lyre* | Ambrosium Shard | +2.5 | +7.5 | Aether |

*Requires specific mod: Better End or Aether

## Unique weapons

### Boss-themed weapons (Tier 5)

Requires **Loot N Explore** mod. These rare weapons drop from specific boss encounters:

**Rapiers**:
- **Dragon's Rapier** (Ender Dragon) - Amethyst Shard repair
- **Coral Rapier** (Elder Guardian) - Prismarine Shard repair
- **Withered Rapier** (Wither) - Bone repair
- **Glacial Rapier** (Ice-themed boss) - Blue Ice repair

All boss rapiers:
- Damage: 6.7
- Rarity: Rare (purple)
- Special: Radiance Melee spell effect
- Loot level: 5

**Lutes**:
- **Dragon Lute** - 10.0 damage, +5.0 Arcane/Healing

**Lyres**:
- **Siren's Lyre** (Elder Guardian) - +2.5 Arcane, +7.5 Healing

### Named unique weapons

Requires **Arsenal** mod. Ultra-rare weapons with unique properties:

- **Singing Blade** (Rapier) - Gold Block repair
- **Lute of Ruby Verdict** - Redstone Block repair, +5.0 both schools
- **Spellthief's Lute** - Iron Block repair, +5.0 both schools
- **Lyre of Apollo** - Gold Block repair, +2.5 Arcane, +7.5 Healing
- **Lyre of Antecael** - Gold Block repair, +2.5 Arcane, +7.5 Healing

All named uniques:
- Rarity: Rare (purple)
- Special: Radiance Melee spell effect
- Loot level: 5

## Progression tips

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start with Tier 1">
    Craft Entertainer's Garb and a Wooden Lute or Golden Lyre. This provides a solid foundation for early game Bard play.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Advance to Tier 2">
    When you have diamonds, upgrade to Troubadour's Garb and Diamond Lute/Lyre/Rapier. This significantly increases your spell power.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Push to Tier 3">
    Netherite Troubadour's Garb and Netherite weapons are your endgame baseline. The +100% spell power and +20% movement speed make a huge difference.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Install optional mods for Tier 4-5">
    - **Better Nether**: Ruby equipment
    - **Better End**: Aeternium equipment
    - **Aether**: Valkyrie/Angelic equipment
    - **Armory RPGs**: Storyteller armor (Tier 5)
    - **Loot N Explore**: Boss weapons
    - **Arsenal**: Named unique weapons
  </Step>

  <Step title="Hunt for uniques">
    Farm bosses and high-level loot for rare Tier 5 weapons with special effects.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Choosing your weapon path

### For melee-focused Bards
Progress through Rapiers: Golden → Iron → Diamond → Netherite → Ruby/Aeternium/Valkyrie

### For balanced spellcasters
Progress through Lutes: Wooden → Diamond → Netherite → Ruby/Angelic

### For dedicated healers
Progress through Lyres: Golden → Diamond → Netherite → Aeternium/Valkyrie

### Mixing weapon types
You can switch between weapon types at any tier! Consider carrying:
- A Lute for damage phases
- A Lyre for healing phases
- A Rapier for melee situations

## Material requirements summary

**Common materials**:
- Wool (T1 armor)
- Iron Ingots (T1 weapons, T1 lute)
- Gold Ingots (T0 weapons)
- Diamonds (T2 equipment)
- Netherite Ingots (T3 equipment, T5 armor)

**Mod-specific materials**:
- Nether Ruby (Better Nether)
- Aeternium Ingot (Better End)
- Ambrosium Shard (Aether)
- Various boss materials (Loot N Explore, Arsenal)
