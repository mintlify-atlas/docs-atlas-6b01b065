## Welcome to Bards RPG

Bards RPG is a Minecraft mod that introduces a unique musical magic class to your adventure. As a Bard, you'll wield instruments of power, cast melodic spells, and support your allies through the art of music.

## Your first steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Craft your spell book">
    Begin your journey by crafting a **Bard Spell Book**. This essential item allows you to learn and cast Bard spells.
    
    The spell book grants access to four core Bard spells:
    - **Troubadour's Minuet** - Reduces damage taken
    - **Magical Ballad** - Deals arcane damage and buffs allies
    - **Encore** - Healing and support magic
    - **Army's Paeon** - Buffs nearby allies when you damage enemies
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose your weapon type">
    Bards have access to three distinct weapon categories, each serving a different playstyle:
    
    ### Rapiers
    Fast, elegant swords that can cast spells on hit.
    - Attack speed: -2.0
    - Playstyle: Melee combat with spell casting
    - Start with: **Golden Rapier** or **Iron Rapier**
    
    ### Lutes
    Damage-focused staves that balance arcane and healing power.
    - Attack speed: -3.0
    - Spell Power: +3.5 Arcane and Healing (T1)
    - Playstyle: Balanced offensive spellcaster
    - Start with: **Wooden Lute**
    
    ### Lyres
    Healing-focused staves that excel at supporting allies.
    - Attack speed: -2.2
    - Attack damage: 3.0
    - Spell Power: +1.75 Arcane, +5.25 Healing (T1)
    - Playstyle: Dedicated healer and support
    - Start with: **Golden Lyre**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Craft or find your armor">
    Bard armor provides unique bonuses focused on mobility and spell power.
    
    **Entertainer's Garb** (Tier 1):
    - Crafted from wool
    - +15% Arcane and Healing spell power per piece
    - +3% movement speed per piece
    - Full set: +60% spell power, +12% movement speed
    
    You can also find Bard equipment in loot chests throughout your world.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Learn your first spell">
    Open your Bard Spell Book and learn **Magical Ballad**, a versatile tier 2 spell that:
    - Fires a projectile that pierces up to 3 enemies
    - Deals arcane damage (0.75x spell power coefficient)
    - Applies the **Ballad** effect to allies hit
    - **Ballad** buff: Increases attack damage and spell power by 2.5% per stack
    - Cooldown: 10 seconds
    - Exhaust: 0.2
  </Step>

  <Step title="Practice channeling">
    Many Bard spells use channeling mechanics. When casting **Magical Ballad**:
    - Hold the cast button for up to 4 seconds (20 ticks)
    - You can move at 1.5x normal speed while channeling
    - Music note particles appear at your feet
    - Release to launch the projectile
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding Bard mechanics

### Dual spell schools

Bards uniquely benefit from both **Arcane** and **Healing** spell power:
- Most Bard weapons provide equal bonuses to both schools
- Spells often use arcane for damage and healing for buffs
- This dual nature makes Bards versatile in any party composition

### Status effects

Bards specialize in musical buffs:

**Ballad** - Increases attack damage and spell power by 2.5% per stack (max 3 stacks)

**Troubadour's Minuet** - Reduces damage taken by 5%

**Army's Motivation** - Triggered by damaging enemies with melee, arrows, or spells while Army's Paeon is active

### Movement and mobility

Unlike heavy armor classes, Bard equipment emphasizes speed:
- Each armor piece grants movement speed bonuses
- Channeling spells often allow movement
- Higher tier armor provides even more mobility

## Next steps

Once you've mastered the basics:
- Explore [Progression](/guides/progression) to learn about higher tier equipment
- Check [Configurations](/guides/configurations) to customize your Bard experience
- Experiment with different weapon types to find your playstyle
- Team up with other classes to maximize your support potential

## Tips for new Bards

- **Position carefully**: Bard armor offers low protection, so use your mobility to avoid damage
- **Stack buffs**: Apply Ballad effects to yourself and allies before major fights
- **Mix damage types**: Combine spell damage with melee attacks using rapiers
- **Support first**: In groups, prioritize keeping allies buffed over dealing damage
- **Manage cooldowns**: Plan your spell rotations to maintain constant buff uptime
