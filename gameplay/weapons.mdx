Bards have access to three unique weapon types, each serving a different playstyle: Rapiers for melee combat, Lutes for balanced spell damage, and Lyres for healing-focused support.

## Weapon types

### Rapiers

Rapiers are swift melee weapons that allow Bards to engage in close-quarters combat. They feature faster attack speeds than traditional swords and can be used to cast spells.

**Attack Speed:** -2.0

### Lutes

Lutes are damage-focused staffs that enhance both Arcane and Healing spell power. They are slower to swing but provide substantial spell power bonuses.

**Attack Speed:** -3.0

### Lyres

Lyres are healing-focused staffs that prioritize Healing spell power over Arcane power. They offer a balanced attack speed between Rapiers and Lutes.

**Attack Speed:** -2.2  
**Base Damage:** 3.0

## Weapon comparison

| Weapon Type | Attack Speed | Base Damage | Spell Power Focus |
|-------------|--------------|-------------|-------------------|
| Rapier | -2.0 | 2.1 - 6.7 | None |
| Lute | -3.0 | 4.0 - 10.0 | Balanced (Arcane + Healing) |
| Lyre | -2.2 | 3.0 | Healing-focused |

<Tip>
Choose Rapiers for fast melee combat, Lutes for balanced spell damage, or Lyres if you focus on healing and supporting allies.
</Tip>
