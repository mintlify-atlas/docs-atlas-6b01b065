Bard spells apply unique status effects to allies and enemies. These effects are synchronized across multiplayer and can stack for increased potency.

## Effect mechanics

<Note>
All Bard status effects are marked as synchronized, meaning they properly display and function in multiplayer environments. Effects can stack multiple times based on spell power.
</Note>

### Stacking system

Bard effects use an amplifier cap system:
- Base amplifier is determined by the spell
- Maximum stacks scale with Healing spell power
- Each stack increases the effect's potency
- Stacks refresh duration when reapplied

## Beneficial effects

These effects benefit the target, typically allies or the caster.

### Ballad

<Accordion title="Effect overview">
  **Type:** Beneficial  
  **Color:** Blue-Purple (0x9999ff)  
  **Applied by:** Magical Ballad spell  
  **Description:** Increases Attack Damage & Spell Power
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Effect modifiers">
  **Attack Damage Bonus:**
  - Value: **+2.5% per amplifier**
  - Operation: Multiplicative (ADD_MULTIPLIED_BASE)
  - Attribute: `minecraft:generic.attack_damage`
  
  **Spell Power Bonus:**
  - Value: **+2.5% per amplifier**
  - Operation: Multiplicative (ADD_MULTIPLIED_BASE)
  - Attribute: Generic Spell Power (affects all spell schools)
  
  At amplifier 1 (1 stack), you gain +2.5% to both stats.  
  At amplifier 3 (3 stacks), you gain +7.5% to both stats.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Stack details">
  - **Base duration:** 8 seconds
  - **Base amplifier:** 1
  - **Max stacks:** 3 (base)
  - **Scaling:** Amplifier cap increases with Healing spell power (0.15× multiplier)
  
  With high Healing power, you can exceed 3 stacks for even greater bonuses.
</Accordion>

<Tip>
Ballad is the primary offensive buff for Bards. Keep it stacked on damage dealers to significantly boost their output.
</Tip>

### Troubadour's Minuet

<Accordion title="Effect overview">
  **Type:** Beneficial  
  **Color:** Blue-Purple (0x9999ff)  
  **Applied by:** Troubadour's Minuet spell  
  **Description:** Reduces damage taken
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Effect modifiers">
  **Damage Taken Reduction:**
  - Value: **-5% per amplifier**
  - Operation: Multiplicative (ADD_MULTIPLIED_BASE)
  - Attribute: `spell_engine:damage_taken`
  
  At amplifier 1 (1 stack), you take 5% less damage.  
  At amplifier 3 (3 stacks), you take 15% less damage.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Stack details">
  - **Base duration:** 10 seconds
  - **Base amplifier:** 1
  - **Max stacks:** 3 (base)
  - **Scaling:** Amplifier cap increases with Healing spell power (0.1× multiplier)
</Accordion>

<Tip>
Troubadour's Minuet is essential for survivability. Stack it on tanks or fragile allies to keep them alive during tough fights.
</Tip>

### Army's Paeon

<Accordion title="Effect overview">
  **Type:** Beneficial (Stash)  
  **Color:** Blue-Purple (0x9999ff)  
  **Applied by:** Army's Paeon spell  
  **Description:** The player buffs nearby allies if he damages enemies with melee hits, arrows or spells
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Effect modifiers">
  This is a stash effect with no direct attribute modifiers. It stores spell data that triggers when specific conditions are met.
  
  **Trigger Conditions:**
  - Melee attack impact
  - Arrow hit
  - Damaging spell impact (Active type only)
  
  **Behavior:**
  - Does not consume stash on trigger (consume = 0)
  - Casts a separate spell when triggered
  - Triggers affect nearby allies based on the spell's area
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Stack details">
  - **Base duration:** 8 seconds
  - **Base amplifier:** 1
  - **Max stacks:** 3 (base)
  - **Scaling:** Amplifier cap increases with Healing spell power (0.15× multiplier)
</Accordion>

<Note>
Army's Paeon itself doesn't provide direct stats. It enables the Army's Motivation effect when you damage enemies.
</Note>

### Army's Motivation

<Accordion title="Effect overview">
  **Type:** Beneficial  
  **Color:** Blue-Purple (0x9999ff)  
  **Applied by:** Army's Paeon triggers  
  **Description:** With each melee-, arrow- & spell-hit you deal magic damage, according to the highest attribute, scaling with the effect amplifier
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Effect modifiers">
  This effect has no direct attribute modifiers but enables custom behavior:
  
  **Custom Handler:** `bards_rpg:armies_paeon_impact`
  
  **Behavior:**
  - Triggered on melee attacks, arrow hits, and damaging spells
  - Deals magic damage based on your highest attribute
  - Damage scales with effect amplifier
  - Creates golden particle burst on each trigger
  - Internal cooldown: **1 second** between triggers
</Accordion>

<Tip>
Alternate between melee, ranged, and spell attacks to maximize Army's Motivation triggers, but remember the 1-second cooldown between activations.
</Tip>

## Maximizing effect stacks

<Steps>
  <Step title="Increase Healing spell power">
    Equip weapons and armor that boost Healing spell power. Effects like Ballad and Troubadour's Minuet use Healing school for their buff components.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Reapply before expiration">
    Stack refreshes when reapplied. Time your spell casts to maintain maximum stacks throughout combat.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Focus on one effect">
    With proper timing, you can maintain high stacks of a single effect by repeatedly casting the same spell.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Coordinate with allies">
    Multiple Bards can stack the same effects. Coordinate to keep permanent uptime on key buffs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
The amplifier cap multiplier determines how many additional stacks you can gain beyond the base maximum. With 0.15× scaling, every 6.67 points of spell power grants +1 max stack.
</Note>

## Effect interactions

### Multiplicative stacking

All Bard effects use multiplicative bonuses (ADD_MULTIPLIED_BASE operation). This means:

- Effects stack multiplicatively with base stats
- Multiple different effects multiply together
- Scales better with higher base stats

**Example with Ballad:**
- Base Attack Damage: 10
- Ballad (3 stacks): +7.5%
- Final Attack Damage: 10 × 1.075 = 10.75

### Effect duration management

All Bard buff spells have the same cooldown (10 seconds) but different effect durations:

| Spell | Effect Duration | Cooldown | Uptime Potential |
|-------|----------------|----------|------------------|
| Troubadour's Minuet | 10s | 10s | 100% |
| Magical Ballad | 8s | 10s | 80% |
| Army's Paeon | 8s | 10s | 80% |

<Tip>
Troubadour's Minuet can achieve 100% uptime if cast on cooldown, making it excellent for sustained defensive support.
</Tip>
