Bards wear specialized garb that provides protection while enhancing spell power and movement speed. Each armor set grants bonuses to both Arcane and Healing spell schools.

## Armor mechanics

All Bard armor sets provide:
- **Arcane Spell Power** multiplier bonuses per piece
- **Healing Spell Power** multiplier bonuses per piece
- **Movement Speed** multiplier bonuses per piece
- Low physical protection compared to traditional armor
- High enchantability for magical enhancements

<Note>
Bard armor prioritizes mobility and magical power over physical defense. The armor sets grant percentage-based bonuses that scale with your total spell power.
</Note>

## Armor pieces

Each armor set consists of four pieces:

### Helmet (Hat)
- Lowest protection value (1)
- Same spell power and movement bonuses as other pieces
- Lightest piece in each set

### Chestplate (Garb/Tunic)
- Highest protection value (3)
- Same spell power and movement bonuses as other pieces
- Most durable piece in each set

### Leggings (Trousers)
- Medium protection value (2)
- Same spell power and movement bonuses as other pieces

### Boots
- Lowest protection value (1)
- Same spell power and movement bonuses as other pieces
- Essential for maximum movement speed

## Progression guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Early game">
    Craft **Entertainer's Garb** using wool. This set is accessible early and provides a solid foundation of +60% spell power and +12% movement speed.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Mid game">
    Upgrade to **Troubadour's Garb** for +80% spell power and +16% movement speed. The improved enchantability allows for better enchantments.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Late game">
    Obtain **Netherite Troubadour's Garb** by upgrading your Troubadour's set with netherite. This provides +100% spell power, +20% movement speed, and netherite's inherent benefits.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="End game">
    If you have Armory RPGs installed, work toward **Storyteller's Garb** for the ultimate +120% spell power and +24% movement speed bonuses.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
All Bard armor pieces provide the same percentage bonuses regardless of slot, so even wearing just one or two pieces can significantly boost your spell power!
</Tip>
